By unlocking the third dimension, drones enable new possibilities for commerce. But current drone designs are still too loud and disruptive for an urban environment, limiting their operational scale.

To really take off, drones must minimize disruption, and acoustics are a good place to start. So, we’re offering a fast prize of up to $1M for the rapid development of an ultra-silent drone.

The drone we want to see can’t be any louder than 50 dBA when flying 10 ft overhead. It must be able to carry a payload weighing at least 25 percent of its mass, and has to weigh at least 1 lb. And it must not be too expensive to build. Building your own prototype from scratch or modifying an existing drone are both acceptable.

The early application deadline to claim the full $1M payout is June 1st, 2024. Late applications made by September 1st, 2024 would claim a payout of $700K. Any later applications would claim $500K. Anyone living anywhere in the world is welcome to apply.1
